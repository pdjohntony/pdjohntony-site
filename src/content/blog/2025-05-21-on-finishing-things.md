Starting a project feels like the whole thing. The itch, the empty repo, the first commit where everything is still possible. Then week two arrives and there's a settings page to build, and suddenly a new itch appears.

I counted: eleven repos named some variation of "experiment". This post is me thinking through why that keeps happening, and the one rule that's been helping — a project isn't started until I've written down what "done" means.
