There are homelabs with rack mounts, 10-gig networking, and enough compute to render a Pixar film. Mine is three Raspberry Pis in a shoebox on top of the router, and I love it.

It runs DNS, a handful of containers, and my habit tracker. The shoebox has a label that says "do not unplug" facing the wall, where no one can see it. This is called defense in depth.
