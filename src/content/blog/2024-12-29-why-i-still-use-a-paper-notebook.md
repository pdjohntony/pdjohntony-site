I've tried every notes app. I keep coming back to a $4 notebook and whatever pen is closest.

The notebook never gets a redesign, never asks me to log in again, and never suggests an AI summary of my grocery list. It has 100% uptime and a terrible search feature, and I've made my peace with the trade.
