Two weeks, one 28-liter backpack, zero regrets about the packing list. Several regrets about the souvenirs I had to carry home in a paper bag.

The short version: pack half of what you think you need, do laundry twice, and trust the convenience stores with your life. A full gear list and the promised 7-Eleven breakfast ranking are coming once I finish arguing with myself about the egg sandwich.
