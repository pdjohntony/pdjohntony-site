Lisbon in February is quiet, golden, and roughly 40% staircase.

I walked 60,000 steps, ate a pastel de nata at every bakery that would have me, and filled a camera roll with that low winter light bouncing off the tiles. Notes and photos to follow once I've culled the 400 nearly identical tram pictures.
